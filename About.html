About this page
This website was built by Aaron Ratnavel. The intent of the this website is to show everyone the road traffic mortality rates all around the world using data from the World Health Organization. A road traffic death, is simple what it says. It is the number of deaths on the road every year. This problem has been increasing over the years, and this website will show you just how many deaths there are every year in terms of road traffic deaths every year per country.
This website not only shows the worlds road traffic death rate, but it also shows the rest of the world compared to the Canada in terms of average road traffic deaths per year. This feature can easily be accessed by going to the home back then clicking on the “Canada” button. Some other features that Can easily be accessed is the “graph” function. This function displays the country of your choices death rate, using a graph. It can be accessed by going to the home page, then clicking on the "graph" button.
Hopefully after you take a peek at this website you will see just how many road traffic deaths there are every year in every country, and maybe you will be more careful next time you are on the road.
